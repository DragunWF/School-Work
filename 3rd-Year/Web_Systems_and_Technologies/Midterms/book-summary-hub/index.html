Atomic Habits Summary

Author: James Clear

Atomic Habits by James Clear emphasizes how small, consistent changes lead to big results. It introduces the four laws of habit formation—cue, craving, response, and reward—while promoting identity-based habits, habit stacking, and environment design to build good habits and break bad ones.

Deep Work Summary

Author: Cal Newport

Deep Work by Cal Newport highlights the power of focused, distraction-free work in a world of constant interruptions. He contrasts deep work with shallow work and offers strategies like scheduling focus time and minimizing distractions to boost productivity and career success.
